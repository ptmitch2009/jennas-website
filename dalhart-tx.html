Dalhart sits where Dallam and Hartley Counties meet, in the far northwest corner of the Texas Panhandle. This is XIT Ranch country — a landscape defined by vast cattle operations, irrigated farmland, and a community of people who have thrived by being resourceful and self-sufficient. But living in one of the more remote parts of Texas comes with a practical challenge that many Dalhart families face every year: access to affordable, comprehensive health care and the insurance to pay for it.

With the nearest major medical centers located 85 miles away in Amarillo, telehealth is not just a convenience for Dalhart residents — it is a lifeline. Every PPO plan offered through Jenna Trejo includes telemedicine services, giving your family access to doctors around the clock without a long drive across the plains. For routine check-ups, specialist consultations, or after-hours advice, telehealth keeps your family connected to quality care no matter where you are on the ranch.

Beyond telehealth, these private PPO plans offer the same broad networks, flexible deductibles, and significant savings that benefit families across the Panhandle. Whether you run cattle, operate farm equipment, own a business on Dalhart's main strip, or work independently in any trade, Jenna can help you find a health plan that covers your family without draining your finances. She works entirely by phone, so the 85-mile distance from Amarillo is never a barrier to getting the help you need.

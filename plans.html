Full Transparency

These Plans May Not Be Ideal For Everyone

Jenna believes in honest guidance. Here are some situations where a different option might be better.

  • Guaranteed-issue needs: These plans are medically underwritten, so approval is not guaranteed. If you need coverage regardless of health history, an ACA marketplace plan may be more appropriate.
  • Large ACA subsidies: If you qualify for significant marketplace subsidies, your ACA plan might end up costing less. Jenna can help you compare both options.
  • Certain pre-existing conditions: Some health conditions may affect eligibility or coverage terms. Jenna will always be upfront about what to expect.

Not sure which direction is right for you? Jenna offers free consultations with no pressure and no obligation. She'll help you find the best fit, even if it's not one of her plans.

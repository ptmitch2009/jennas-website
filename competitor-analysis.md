# Competitive Analysis: Jenna Cleckler Insurance
## Health Insurance Agents & Brokers - Texas Panhandle / Amarillo Market
### Date: March 24, 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This analysis covers 12 competitors across three tiers:
- **Tier 1 - Direct Local Competitors** (Amarillo-based health insurance agents)
- **Tier 2 - Regional/Statewide Competitors** (Texas-wide agents targeting similar keywords)
- **Tier 3 - National Niche Competitors** (Online brokers targeting PPO/private plan keywords)

**Key finding:** The Amarillo health insurance market is surprisingly weak in digital presence. Most local agents have minimal websites, no blogs, and poor SEO. The biggest competitive threats come from statewide digital-first brokers who are capturing search traffic. Jenna has a significant opportunity to dominate local search with relatively modest effort.

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS FOR JENNA

### Immediate Wins (Low Effort, High Impact)

1. **Claim all local directories:** Ensure Google Business Profile, Yelp, BBB, Expertise.com, Amarillo Chamber, Yellow Pages, and Trusted Choice listings are complete and optimized.

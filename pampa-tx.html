Affordable Health Insurance for Pampa, TX Families & Self-Employed

Dependable PPO coverage for Gray County's ranching families and independent workers — backed by a local agent in nearby Amarillo.

Pampa sits at the heart of Gray County's wide-open ranch country, where agriculture and the energy industry have sustained families for generations. Life here rewards hard work and self-reliance — and that independent spirit extends to how many Pampa residents earn their living. Ranchers, farm operators, independent contractors, and small business owners make up a significant share of the local workforce, and most of them do not have access to employer-sponsored group health insurance.

For these families, finding affordable health coverage can feel like an impossible task. Marketplace plans often come with steep premiums once you exceed the subsidy threshold, and the provider networks can be frustratingly narrow for residents of smaller communities. Private PPO plans offer a practical alternative: flexible deductibles, access to a broad network of doctors and hospitals including those in Amarillo, and monthly costs that are often 20 to 60 percent lower than what the marketplace charges.

Jenna Trejo is a licensed insurance agent in Amarillo — about 60 miles from Pampa — who specializes in helping rural Panhandle families find health plans that actually make sense for their lives. She handles everything by phone so you never have to drive into the city for a meeting, and she takes the time to understand your situation before recommending anything.

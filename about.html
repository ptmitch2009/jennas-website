My Story

I Know What It's Like

Hi, I'm Jenna. I'm a wife, a mom to two amazing kids, and I live right here in Amarillo, Texas. Health insurance wasn't supposed to be my career — but life has a way of pointing you where you're needed most.

I've lived with Type 1 Diabetes my entire life. That means I've spent years sitting in waiting rooms, fighting with insurance companies, staring at bills that didn't make sense, and worrying about whether my coverage would actually be there when I needed it. I know what it feels like to be confused, frustrated, and honestly a little scared about your health coverage.

That experience changed everything for me. I realized there are so many people out there — families, self-employed folks, small business owners — who feel the exact same way. They just want someone to sit down with them, explain their options in plain English, and help them find a plan that actually works for their life and their budget.

That's what I do. I work one-on-one with every single person I help. No call centers, no generic recommendations, no pressure. Just honest, personal guidance from someone who truly gets it.

“I don't just sell insurance. I help people find peace of mind — because I know how much that's worth.”

— Jenna Cleckler

My Promise

You Get More Than a Policy

When you get your coverage through me, you're not just buying a plan and being left to figure things out on your own. You're getting a personal advisor who's in your corner for the long haul.

I'm your main point of contact for questions about your benefits, help with claims, finding in-network providers, and anything else that comes up. I will always treat you like a neighbor, not a number. I'll be honest about what your options are, I'll answer your questions without the jargon, and I'll be here whenever you need me — not just at enrollment time.

Honesty First

Even If I'm Not the Right Fit

Here's something most insurance agents won't tell you: their plans aren't always the best option for everyone. I will.

My goal isn't to make a sale — it's to make sure you end up with the best coverage for your unique situation. If the plans I offer aren't the right match for you, I'll tell you that. I'll help you understand your other options, point you in the right direction, and even walk you through the process of finding coverage elsewhere.

Your health and your family's wellbeing are too important for anything less than the right plan. I'd rather help you find the perfect fit somewhere else than put you in something that doesn't serve you well. That's just how I operate.
